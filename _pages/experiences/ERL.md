# Experience Overview

I participated in an Existential Robotics Lab workshop focused on practical robotics foundations and gave a presentation in the workshop program. My main responsibility was presenting the section on frame transformations and helping connect the math to implementation decisions in robotics pipelines.

# Context and Contributions

- Presented core concepts of coordinate frames and frame transformations.
- Helped explain why transform chains are critical for perception, planning, and control.
- Contributed to technical discussions with participants during the workshop sessions.
